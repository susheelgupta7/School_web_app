Educating India

90% of the schools are located in rural area. Lack of proper transportation to school, basic infrastructure, qualified teachers, quality of education, examination monitoring & proctoring, lack of standardised Assessment Methods, low awareness of importance of education, etc are just small portion of challenges which holds back the development.
At least 35 million children in India aged 6 â€“ 14 years do not attend school. 53% of girls in the age group of 5 to 9 years are illiterate. Only 53% of habitation has a primary school. In India, only 20% of habitation has a secondary school.
We have come up with an idea of turning buses into mobile schools, for the children belonging to the remote areas and who cannot go to school because in the mornings they are busy earning their livelihoods and also for those girls who are denied from educating themselves because of their family's security concerns.
